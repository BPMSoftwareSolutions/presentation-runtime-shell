Event Flow + Insight
Events moved through handlers into orchestration pipelines and then into structured outputs.
Plugins extended analytics, automation, and orchestration without changing the core engine.
The architecture was self-documenting enough to attract contributors and demo extensibility directly.
It also exposed a failure mode: too many layers can turn structure into accidental complexity.
Systems do not only fail from lack of structure. They also fail when the structure becomes the thing you are managing.
